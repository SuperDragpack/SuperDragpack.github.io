About EJ

“EJ” is a 1970 Mustang that retains the soul of a classic Mustang, whilst hiding its true identity as a usable high-performance daily driver.

If it looks original, that’s deliberate . . . but every detail has been engineered to improve drivability

The build followed three simple rules:
1/ The original chassis was to be 100% unmodified
2/ Every part had to enhance the driving experience
3/ 100% designed & built by the owner — design, fabrication, mechanics, paint, electronics, tuning . . .

Engine

Engine

302ci (5.0) V8 block & stock crank
TrickFlow Twisted Wedge heads & intake system
Vortech V2 supercharged (6psi pulley)
Trick Flow Stage 1 Roller Cam with 1.6:1 Roller Rockers

Approx. 500 hp.

Fuel & Ignition

Custom-built standalone Fuel Injection ECU and loom - DIY tuneable with full fuel & ignition control.
380cc HYDA Injectors

Crank Position Sensor based timing
Stock Distributor for spark distribution only

Other Engine Bay Mods:

VW/Skoda Alternator
Alfa 147 Radiator with 156 Cooling Fan (controlled by Dash ECU)
Renault Clio expansion bottle
2x Jeep WJ transmission coolers

Drivetrain

4.44:1 Limited Slip Diff - for instant "go"
87-93 Mustang "AOD" overdrive automatic - for relaxed cruising.
87-93 Mustang Propshaft with custom diff adapter
Speed Sensor via custom Hall Effect sensor inside front wheel hub

Chassis & Suspension

Modern high performance suspension, steering and brakes mounted to the unmodified original chassis locations using custom CAD-designed front and rear subframes. Exceptional handling and comfort without compromise!

Carefully selected OEM parts including:
* 5 link adjustable independant rear suspension
* Double wishbone front suspension
* Performance disc brakes (322mm vented front, 302mm vented rear)
* Programmable speed sensitive Electric Power Steering grafted into original column
* Rack-n-pinion steering rack
* BMW F20 Brake Master Cylinder and Servo (fitted to original bulkhead and pedal via adapter)
etc

Using carefully selected drivetrain components from modern production cars, EJ has the handling and stopping power of a modern sports car, without compromising the original chassis. Custom-designed subframes avoid the heavy structural modifications typical of off-the-shelf kits.

Interior

Largely original in appearance, very modern underneath.

Fully computerised gauges retain the classic analog look whilst hiding an array of digital features . . .

Full leather interior featuring electric and heated seats (and full seat belts), plus electric mirrors (controls hidden within original switchgear) additional lighting and many more secrets behind EJ's classic facade.

* BMW 4Series Convertible front seats with custom ECUs to retain electric functions and heaters (and full seat belts)
* BMW 4Series Convertible rear seats blended onto original seat frames to fit vehicle chassis without modifications
* Additional gauges, USB charger and discreet controls

Custom Electronics

Bespoke electronics architecture using modern microcontrollers. Analogue appearance, digital brains.

Custom designed, built and programmed microcontroller ECU's provide unique processing power for a classic ride.

Various ECU's monitor, control and display a full array of parameters plus provide unique comforts including full electric memory seats with heaters, custom dash lighting, tunable steering weight (at pre-set speeds), engine cooling programs and much more.
