About EJ

EJ is a 1970 Mustang restomod built with a simple brief: retain the soul of a classic Mustang, while quietly fixing the bits Ford hadn’t worked out yet. It is not a restoration, and it is not a trailer ornament.

The approach throughout is OEM-plus. If it looks original, that’s deliberate. If it works better than original, that’s also deliberate.

Engine

Base

Ford small-block V8, Fox-body based 5.0-litre architecture.

Fuel & Ignition

Modern electronic fuel injection and mapped ignition for reliability and drivability.

Management

Speeduino standalone ECU, DIY built and tuned.

Drivetrain

Chosen for strength, availability and real-world usability rather than headline numbers. Built to cope with torque, miles and misuse.

Chassis & Suspension

Updated suspension geometry and components improve control without changing the character of the car. Electric power steering is speed-sensitive and tuned for feel, not novelty.

Interior

Largely original in appearance, quietly modern underneath. Accurate gauges, sensible ergonomics and everything works — on purpose.

Custom Electronics

Bespoke electronics architecture using modern microcontrollers. Analogue hardware, digital brains, no unnecessary screens.
