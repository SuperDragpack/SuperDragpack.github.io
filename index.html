About EJ

“EJ” is a 1970 Mustang that retains the soul of a classic Mustang, whilst making it a usable daily driver.

If it looks original, that’s deliberate . . .
but every single detail has been carefully designed to enhance its drivability.

The build followed three simple rules:
1/ The original chassis was to be 100% unmodified
2/ Every part had to enhance the driving experience
3/ 100% designed, built and installed by the owner — including paint, electronics and mechanics.

Engine

Engine

Ford 5.0 / 302 V8 with Trick Flow heads and intake, Vortech V2 supercharger. Approx. 500 hp.

Fuel & Ignition

Custom-built standalone EFI ECU and loom. Designed, built and tuned by the owner.

Cooling

ECU-controlled custom engine and transmission cooling system.

Drivetrain

4.54:1 LSD drag gearing paired with an overdrive automatic for effortless motorway cruising.

Chassis & Suspension

CAD-designed front and rear subframes mounted exclusively using original fixing locations. High-performance OEM suspension components provide exceptional ride and comfort without compromise.

Interior

Largely original in appearance, quietly modern underneath. Fully digitally controlled gauges and modern comforts including electric and heated seats, electric mirrors and more.

Custom Electronics

Bespoke electronics architecture using modern microcontrollers. Analogue appearance, digital brains.
